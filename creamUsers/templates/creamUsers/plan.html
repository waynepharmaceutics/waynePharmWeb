Cream Base

Every skin product consists of an oil phase and water phase. This is true whether you are talking about a cream, ointment, or lotion. Getting the ingredients right in the base are essential to the overall effectiveness of the skin product.

Oil Phase

Depending on what oil and how much can greatly vary the thickness and density of the resulting cream. We currently use a mixture containing either glycerin or propylene glycol depending on user specifications.

Water Phase

No regular water here. Boiled. Condensed. Distilled. Pure.

Main Components

Here is where we put all the ingredients together to make something just for you. Unlike some other brands out there. What we put in, is at a level high enough to actually do what it's supposed to.

Caffeine

Like a cup of coffee wakes you up, so caffeine does to your skin as well. Caffeine helps to tighten up skin a little bit, reducing signes of lines and wrinkles

Cocoa Butter

Giant chunks of Cocoa Butter. Excellent emollient for your skin. Softens and smoothes. Like Butter.

Collagen

Collagen is a protein that helps your cells stick together. What it does for your skin is increase elasticity and help improve the structure.

Vitamin A

Retinol, or Vitamin A. What doesn't it do? From reducing fine lines to protecting cells from harmful UV rays, this vitamin ticks every box. We put a lot of this in your product.

Finishing Touches

The last steps, where we make sure everything stays safe and stable for you to use.

Preservative

As much as we don't like it, a little bit is necessary to make sure nothing harmful can make it's way into your product. We use the bare minimum it takes to help ensure your safety.
